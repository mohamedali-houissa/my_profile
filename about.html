ABOUT US

Beyond Retro has been created in 2021 by 4 students, coming from IESEG Paris. We are all four passionated about fashion and styling. We have at heart to raise awareness about the dangers of fast fashion, and to encourage small sustainable business and second hand stores. We have created a real universe for the brand, an environment in which customers feel comfortable. We want customers to reinvent themselves through Beyond Retro.
